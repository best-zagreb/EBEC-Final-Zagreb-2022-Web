WHAT IS EBEC

EBEC (European BEST Engineering Competition) is an engineering competition made by students – for students and is held in 21 countries across Europe. EBEC has three levels – the local level winner gets to goes on to the regional round, and the top 10 Europe teams in each category can show off their skills at the final level which will be once again held in Zagreb after 10 years.
