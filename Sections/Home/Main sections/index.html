What is EBEC

European BEST Engineering Competition (EBEC) is a 3 level, team-based engineering competition made by students – for students. It is held in 19 countries across Europe in 2 categories – Innovative or Team Design and Case Study .

The top 10 Europe teams in each category can show off their skills at the final level which will be held in Zagreb for the first time in 10 years.

EBEC Final

EBEC project begins every year with the Local Rounds of EBEC followed by National/Regional Rounds, and reaches its end in the Final Round at the end of July.
The winning teams from Local Rounds progress to National/Regional Rounds and the winning teams from National and Regional Rounds are competing in the Final Round of EBEC.

The team which provides the most efficient, creative and successful solution at the end of the Final Round of EBEC is awarded the BEST Engineers of Europe title.

Partners

Partners are a big part of every EBEC competition, especially EBEC Final. This is why we are proud to present our partners for EBEC Final Zagreb 2022. Find out more about our partners on the PARTNERS page!
