Competition place - Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing 📚

Tag: Info

Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing, commonly known as FER, is a four building complex in which the competition will be taking place.
FER is the largest technical faculty and the leading educational as well as research-and-development institution in the fields of electrical engineering and computing in Croatia with over 600 new students every academic year. 👨‍🏫

Accomodation in SD ‘Sava’ 🏡

Tag: Info

It's time to reveal where all are participants will be hosted - Student dormitory Stjepan Radić!
SD Stjepan Radić is a home to over 3500 students 🛏️ during the academic year. Its close proximity to the town center, Jarun lake 🏐 and many other facilities together with the integrated messrooms, cafeteria and pastry shop 🍰, cinema and fitness center make it the go to place for students from all over Zagreb! 🎉
